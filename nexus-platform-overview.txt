WHAT IS NEXUS?
--------------
Nexus is a white-label programmatic SEO platform built exclusively for
marketing agencies. It automates the creation, hosting, scoring, and
management of thousands of location-specific service pages — pages like
"Credit Card Processing in Austin, TX" — so agencies can scale local SEO
for their clients without scaling headcount.

Agencies manage all their clients from one dashboard. Each client gets
their own branded presence, served on their own domain, with no Nexus
branding visible to the public.


THE CORE PROMISE
----------------
"Scale Local SEO Without Scaling Headcount."

Nexus lets agencies deliver human-quality, AI-speed content — turning
one service + one location list into tens of thousands of unique,
indexable pages that rank in local searches.


HOW IT WORKS (CORE WORKFLOW)
-----------------------------
1. Agency connects a client website and sets up their brand profile,
   services, and target locations.
2. Nexus AI generates unique content variations for 8 key page sections
   (Hero, Intro, Why Choose Us, Process, FAQ, Trust Signals, CTA, Footer).
3. The Bulk Generator combines services × locations to produce thousands
   of unique pages instantly.
4. Every page is automatically scored 0–100 for quality and assigned a tier.
5. Only Tier 1 and Tier 2 pages make it into the sitemap.
6. Top Tier 1 pages are submitted directly to the Google Indexing API.
7. Underperforming pages are automatically demoted. Digests go to clients
   every Monday at 8 AM.


KEY FEATURES
============


1. WHITE-LABEL MULTI-TENANT DASHBOARD
--------------------------------------
- Manage unlimited client websites from one admin panel
- Each client has their own brand profile, voice, tone, and color settings
- No Nexus branding exposed to end clients or the public
- Role-based access: agency admin and client views


2. CLOUDFLARE WORKER PROXY
---------------------------
- Client pages are served directly on the client's own domain
  (e.g., client.com/pages/plumbing-in-austin-tx)
- Sub-100ms response times globally via Cloudflare's edge network
- No separate hosting setup required for the client


3. AI CONTENT ENGINE
---------------------
- Multi-provider AI failover (OpenAI → Anthropic → Perplexity)
- Generates 8 unique content sections per page:
    • Hero / headline
    • Intro / overview
    • Why Choose Us
    • Our Process
    • FAQ (with schema markup)
    • Trust Signals / Social Proof
    • Call-to-Action
    • Footer copy
- Content is stored in Variation Banks — a library of AI-written snippets
  that are mixed and rotated to ensure no two pages are identical


4. BULK PAGE GENERATOR
-----------------------
- Select services + locations → click Generate → get thousands of pages
- Blueprint templates (HTML) can be customized per industry
- Background job processing with real-time progress tracking
- "Run in Background" option — leave the dialog and check back later
- Pages auto-save and auto-publish based on quality score


5. QUALITY SCORING SYSTEM
--------------------------
- Every page is scored 0–100 automatically on generation
- Scoring criteria: content depth, uniqueness, keyword relevance,
  internal link density, FAQ presence, schema markup
- Tier assignment:
    • Tier 1 (score 80+): included in sitemap + Google Indexing API
    • Tier 2 (score 50–79): included in sitemap, crawlable
    • Tier 3 (score <50): excluded from sitemap, no-indexed
- Threshold is adjustable per website


6. DYNAMIC PAGE GENERATION
---------------------------
- Pages that haven't been explicitly generated are served on-the-fly
  from variation banks when a visitor or bot hits the URL
- Location intelligence: supports city, state, and city+state formats
- Canonical redirect: abbreviated URLs redirect to full canonical slugs
- On-the-fly pages are cached in memory (10-min TTL) for performance


7. SITEMAP MANAGER
-------------------
- Automatically builds and maintains XML sitemaps
- Only Tier 1 and Tier 2 pages are included
- Sitemaps regenerate automatically after tier changes
- Ping to Google on sitemap update


8. GOOGLE INDEXING API INTEGRATION
------------------------------------
- Direct integration with Google's Indexing API via service account
- New Tier 1 pages are submitted for indexing automatically
- Manual "Request Indexing" available per page
- Google Search Console (GSC) connection for impression/click data


9. GOOGLE SEARCH CONSOLE INTEGRATION
--------------------------------------
- Connect GSC per website via service account
- View impressions, clicks, CTR, and average position per page
- Data pulled directly into the Nexus dashboard
- Works even when a site has zero impressions yet (connection verified
  via permissions check, not data presence)


10. INTERNAL LINK ENGINE
--------------------------
- Automatically identifies and inserts contextual internal links
  between related pages (e.g., service hub → city pages)
- Distributes PageRank across the page graph
- Configurable link density per page


11. AUTOMATION PIPELINE (8 AUTOMATED WORKFLOWS)
-------------------------------------------------
Auto 1: Score pages immediately after generation
Auto 2: Assign tiers based on score thresholds
Auto 3: Regenerate sitemap after tier changes
Auto 4: Submit new Tier 1 pages to Google Indexing API
Auto 5: Promote high-traffic dynamic pages to permanent pages
Auto 6: Auto-demote Tier 1 pages with zero impressions over threshold
Auto 7: Weekly SEO health digest emailed to clients every Monday 8 AM
Auto 8: Launch health scoring — flags sites that aren't ready to launch


12. LEAD TRACKING & ROI DASHBOARD
-----------------------------------
- Built-in contact form tracking on every published page
- Leads appear in the Leads & ROI dashboard in real time
- AI-powered lead qualification: Hot / Warm / Cold scoring
- AI drafts an email reply for each lead automatically
- "Book Job" button links leads to a booking calendar
- "Booked ✓" badge prevents double-booking
- Lead source tracking: which page and which service drove the lead
- Revenue attribution: link booked jobs to dollar value for ROI reporting


13. CALL TRACKING
------------------
- Unique tracking phone numbers per campaign
- Calls logged and attributed to the source page
- Call duration and outcome tracking


14. HUB PAGES
--------------
- State-level and service-level hub pages that aggregate city pages
- Auto-generated navigation linking hub → city pages
- Bulk generate hub pages across all states in one click
- Background processing with auto-refresh on completion


15. LAUNCH GOVERNOR & SAFETY RAILS
------------------------------------
- Pre-launch checklist: domain connected, brand profile complete,
  variation banks ready, minimum page count met
- Launch Health Score per website (0–100)
- Prevents publishing if critical requirements aren't met
- Churn reduction alerts: flags at-risk accounts based on activity


16. ONBOARDING AUTOMATION
--------------------------
- Post-payment onboarding funnel tied to Stripe
- When a customer pays, Nexus auto-creates their account, website,
  and brand profile
- Onboarding form collects brand details, services, and target locations
- Admin Onboarding Test Tool to simulate the full flow


17. ANALYTICS DASHBOARD
------------------------
- Dual-mode: aggregate (all pages) and per-page views
- Impressions, clicks, CTR, average position (from GSC)
- Page-level performance breakdown
- Fallback hit tracking (which dynamic slugs are being requested)
- Promotion threshold tracking (which dynamic pages should become permanent)


18. ADMIN PANEL
----------------
- Full platform admin at /admin
- Manage all agencies, websites, users, and accounts
- Override-publish individual pages
- Bulk operations: score, tier, publish, generate
- Notification center for system events
- API usage log across AI providers
